## Become a PAA Observer, win some free stuff.

PAA is always having tournaments and looking for official observers to participate in the tournaments. This is a chance to land some free gear!


Plus watch some awesome pro bass fisherman practice.

## Why You would Do this?

### Learn new tips not available to the general public straight from the pros as they're using the technique.

https://youtu.be/1m-CRHHw9Eo

Home of the Professional Anglers Association: [PAA](https://npaa.net/)

Check out their [list of guides](https://npaa.net/guide-directory/) that are Coast Guard Certified and true fishing professionals.
