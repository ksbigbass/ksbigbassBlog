## MLF: Lake Jordan, Raleigh NC Big Fish Galore

MLF: Lake Jordan, Raleigh NC Big Fish Galore! Wow! Over 82 Pounds in Shotgun Round 1 for Pro Kelly Jordon. KJ shows us just how many big fish are in Lake Jordan, NC.



[Jordan reservoir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jordan_Lake) covers 13,940 acres with a shoreline of 180 miles.

## Prespawn

The fish are in a pre-spawn mode at Lake Jordan, and the pros are finding them in conventional pre-spawn locations.

[MLF Pro Kelly Jordon](https://majorleaguefishing.com/angler/kelly-jordon/) is catching his fish shallow. 1-4ft. Water temps are reported to be 55-60 degrees maybe warmer in the back of the creeks.

## Back of Creeks

### Targeting creek channels and the edge of creeks with a slight depth change leading to a spawning flat.
