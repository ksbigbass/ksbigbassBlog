MLF Bass Pro Tour Favorite Fishing Stage 3 has come to a close on [Shearon Harris Reservoir](http://www.wakegov.com/parks/harrislake/Pages/default.aspx) with an exciting period of fish catch after fish catch. 


[Jacob Powroznik’s](https://majorleaguefishing.com/angler/jacob-powroznik/) is sponsored by Quicksilver, Lowrance, Livingston Lures, TH Marine, Ranger Boats, Bass Mafia, MotorGuide.


Jacob reported he couldn't see the spawning fish due to cloud cover during the championship round in Raleigh, NC. Lakes catching nine fish in the third period, adding more than 32 pounds. His fish was caught on grass that is found in spots in the lake he had found 5 years ago.

## This tournament he Caught two fish over 8 lbs in 1 day - first for him.

### Caught his fish on a V&M ChopStick worm


Powroznik's 5 inch V&M Baits Chopstick wacky rigged on a #1 Mustad TitanX Rig/Neko Hook.
