RTD: Rod Threading Device: Fishing Line Rod Threading Tool uses spring-loaded arms that take turns gripping the line gripper as it travels thru the line guides. The number of guides could be unlimited. May not work with all micro-guided rods.

### That's it you're done.

Fluorocarbon fishing lines are so small these days just holding onto the line can be difficult. Then the added task of making it through all the guides without dropping it and having to start all over seems impossible some days.

#### Who Needs This Tool.

This little fishing line tool the RTD rod threading device is a savior to people with limited dexterity in your fingers from arthritis.

#### People With Dexterity Issues.

Sight Issues: Eliminates the Need to See the Line.

Saves Time. String a rod in seconds.

This tool makes threading your fishing line through your rods eyelets quick and easy.

#### Easily to use.

The RTD threads all types line on fishing rods in mere seconds. Fast and easy to use RTD works with most traditional rod types and also works awesome on fly fishing rods or spey rods.
