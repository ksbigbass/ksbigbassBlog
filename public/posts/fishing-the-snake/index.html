Fishing the Snake

March 28, 2019

Fishing the Snake River

Fishing the Snake: Fishing in PSNW for steelhead. With guide Trevor Wine from WCA fly Fishing we headed out early in a well equipped drift boat and ready to catch some giant trout.

First Trout from the Snake River

My first fish from the Snake wasn’t very big but it did get me excited! I can’t believe how much effort goes into just getting a bite. The Snake River is a barbless sanctuary which makes things just a little bit tougher on us the anglers and better for protecting the fishery.

All my fish we released after pictures and of course a kiss.
