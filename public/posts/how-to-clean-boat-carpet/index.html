How To: Clean Boat Carpet

March 16, 2019

Cleaning your bass boat marine carpet, how to get the blood, grease, and slime out. The latest trend in carpet cleaning is here and it might not be what your thinking.

Shaving cream is an effective, inexpensive fix for stained and dirty boat carpet.

Barbasol boat carpet cleaning

How To: Clean Boat Carpet

Things you need to get started

  • 2 to 3 cans of Barbasol shaving cream
  • 1 scrub brush
  • Shop-vac or a rented carpet cleaner

Barbasol brand seems to be important for the best results.

Using a Scrub Brush

  • Use your drill with a scrub brush attachment for the improved cleaning ability.

carpet brush for drill

Vigorously scrub a small area of the boat’s carpet with Barbasol shaving cream!

Barbasol shaving cream ex3

Rinse Well!

This part is important!

Be careful using a high powered pressure washer from the local car wash, getting too aggressive can harm the glue underneath creating worse problems later.

Barbasol Ranger bass boat carpet cleaning ex2

Vacuum up!

  • Vacuum dry.
  • Using a shop vac or a rented carpet cleaner is very important to get a great final product.
  • Do your best to rinse all the shaving cream out of the carpet, the residue will create a dirty sticky mess later if not all taken care of.

Mix fabric softener and water in a spray bottle.Â 

  • Wait for the carpet to completely dry, then spray the carpet with the fabric softener.
  • Afterward, lightly go against the grain of the carpet with a pet brush to make it stand back up as if new and smelling fresh.
