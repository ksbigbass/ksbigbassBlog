OMG! Smallie!!!!

May 26, 2011

OMG! Smallie!!!! Was lucky enough to film an incredible smallie show this past week where we had several fish over 6 lbs and 5 fish we weighed over 5 lbs on a digital scale, all on a variety of baits from jerkbaits, jigs, cranks, and lipless cranks.

Best Bait was the sour grape rouge, and football head jigs. I love big smallies.

HUGE Smallmouth

This fish tipped our digital scale at 6.4 lbs of course we released all the fish. The Conservation Series nets are in a class of their own!
